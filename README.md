## Improving Health Outcomes Through Patient Data
Imagine a world in which researchers use real world data to understand diseases and develop better treatments or drugs. A world in which health data from all sources helps physicians and nurses make critical decisions. A world in which patients receive the best treatment tailored to their specific situation and genomic profile based on complete and accurate data.

I have a Master's degree in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science from RWTH Aachen University and a Master of Business Administration from Duke University's Fuqua School of Business. I started my career in oncology on the Gemcitabene pre-launch team at Lilly in Indianapolis.

Today, I am the Senior Vice President for Precision Medicine, Real World Evidence & Analytics at Elsevier and passionate about using health data to drive innovation in healthcare and outcomes for patients.

These repos represent the projects I do after work, hoping they are helpful for others in the field.
